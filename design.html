How to get the best results

Start with overall footprint and wall positions. Don’t worry about furniture first.
Check dimensions (mm) and ceiling heights early.
Place doors and windows where you want light & airflow.
Add key rooms: bedrooms, kitchen triangle, bathrooms, laundry.
